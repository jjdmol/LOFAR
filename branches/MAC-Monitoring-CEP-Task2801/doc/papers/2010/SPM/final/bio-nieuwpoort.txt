Rob V. van Nieuwpoort is a postdoc at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam
and ASTRON.  His current research interests focus on many-core systems
and radio-astronomy.  He got his Ph.D at the Vrije Universiteit
Amsterdam on efficient Java-centric grid computing. He has designed
and implemented the Manta, Ibis, Satin, and JavaGAT systems, and
worked on the GridLab and Virtual Labs for E-science projects. At
ASTRON, he works on the central, real-time data processing of LOFAR.
His research interests include high-performance computing, parallel
and distributed algorithms, networks, programming languages, and
compiler construction.
